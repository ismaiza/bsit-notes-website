Computer Networking

1. Introduction to Computer Networking

Computer Networking is the interconnection of computers and devices to share data, resources, and information.

2. Advantages of Computer Networks

  • Resource sharing
  • Fast communication
  • Cost reduction
  • Centralized data management
  • Improved reliability

3. Types of Networks

  • LAN (Local Area Network): Covers small area
  • MAN (Metropolitan Area Network): Covers city
  • WAN (Wide Area Network): Covers large geographical area

4. Network Topologies

  • Bus Topology
  • Star Topology
  • Ring Topology
  • Mesh Topology
  • Hybrid Topology

5. Network Devices

  • Hub: Broadcasts data to all devices
  • Switch: Sends data to specific device
  • Router: Connects different networks
  • Modem: Converts digital to analog signals
  • Access Point: Provides wireless access

6. Transmission Media

6.1 Guided Media

  • Twisted Pair Cable
  • Coaxial Cable
  • Optical Fiber Cable

6.2 Unguided Media

  • Radio Waves
  • Microwaves
  • Infrared
